Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password." Enter your email address and we'll send you a link to reset your password.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers.

How can I upgrade my plan?

You can upgrade your plan at any time from your account dashboard. Go to "Billing" and select "Upgrade Plan" to see available options.

Where can I find documentation?

Our complete documentation is available in the "Resources" section of your account. You can also visit our documentation portal at docs.yourcompany.com.
